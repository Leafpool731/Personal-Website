About Me

Hello there! My name is Sierra, and I am the author and creator if "I Swear to You". I've been captivated by the art of storytelling since I was just seven years old. From the moment I picked up a pen and filled my first notebook with scribbles of imaginative tales, I knew that writing was my true passion.

As I grew older, my love for crafting narratives only deepened, and I dedicated myself to honing my skills as a wordsmith. Whether it was weaving intricate plots, sculpting vibrant characters, or painting vivid worlds with my words, I have always enjoyed this craft. You can view some of my older stories here!

My current work, "I Swear to You", is a testament to my lifelong dedication to storytelling (and a couple friends). I have been working on this piece since I was in middle school, and it has gone through many iterations. After viewing the Netflix show "Arcane", I knew exactly the type of story this would be.

My goal is to have a final manuscript by the end of 2024! In the mean time, you can check out the cast and magical world of "I Swear to You" here!
