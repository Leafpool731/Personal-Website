Biography

Elize Haillet was born on the outskirts of the enchanted city of Arcanum, far removed from the opulence and splendor of its central districts. Her family lived in a modest cottage on the edge of the mystical forests that surrounded the city, eking out a humble existence away from the bustling streets and towering spires.

From a young age, Elize displayed an innate talent for potion-making, a skill that set her apart from her peers. While other children played in the fields and explored the woods, Elize spent her days poring over alchemical texts and experimenting with ingredients she gathered from the forest.

Elize's family life was fraught with tension and discord. Her parents, struggling to make ends meet, often argued about money and the family's future. Feeling overlooked and undervalued, Elize sought solace in her passion for potion-making, finding refuge in the quiet solitude of her makeshift laboratory.

As she continued to hone her skills and pursue her passion for potion-making, Elize remained determined to prove her worth and forge her own path in the world. With her brother by her side and her unwavering devotion to her craft, she faced the challenges that lay ahead with courage and resilience, determined to make her mark on the world of alchemy.
