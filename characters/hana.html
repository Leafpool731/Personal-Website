Hana

Biography

Hana Arts is the daughter of Lucius Arts from his first marriage, making her the elder half-sister of Scarlet. Born into privilege in the enchanting city of Arcanum, Hana's childhood was marked by the opulence of Arts Manor and the legacy of her father's potion-making empire. Despite the luxuries surrounding her, Hana felt a sense of isolation as the only child from her father's previous marriage.

Growing up, Hana was drawn to the intricate art of potion-making, a skill she inherited from her father. While she excelled in her studies and apprenticeship under her father's guidance, she yearned for a deeper connection with her family, particularly her elusive younger half-sister, Scarlet.
